Season Direction

Spring/Summer 2027 places renewed reverence on craftsmanship and intentional design. Across apparel and accessories, this translates to softorial tailoring, investment-worthy leather goods, the return of the statement tie, a breakout headwear moment, and hardware finishes from vintage patina to sandblasted matte — all bridging heritage with bold modernity.

Softorial Tailoring
Unstructured cotton suits and relaxed linen blazers replace rigid tailoring. Wide-leg trousers at a higher rise mean belts are visible and essential. Accessories need to work with soft proportions.
Wide Silhouettes Are Default
The wide-leg trouser dominates across all lifestyles — from relaxed chinos to pleated dress pants. Belt widths, wallet formats, and tie proportions all respond to these looser, more generous cuts.
Denim Renaissance
Mineral-washed lightweight denim, sashiko-inspired craft, and sculpted forms drive a new denim moment. Belts, leather goods, and accessories must speak to this elevated casual narrative.
Headwear Renaissance
Statement hats are up 140% YoY. The flat-top baseball cap nods to skewed classics while packable straw hats lead travel-friendly styling. Brimless summer hats in crochet raffia evolve the docker beanie for warm weather.
Lifestyle Fluidity
The same accessory works across Dress, Refined Casual, and Casual. Design for versatility — one belt, three occasions. Ties are back (+73% TikTok), officewear surges (+131%), and bags shift from carry to style statement.

01 — The Artisan

Relaxed craft,
sun-drenched soul

NuBohème imperfections define this narrative — woven belts, textured leathers, and vintage-finish hardware for the man who moves between a seaside dinner and weekend markets.

Key Takeaways

Design with intention

Honor the Maker
Braided belts with double-ring closures and elevated knots. Hand-dyed marbled wallets in veg-tan. Crafted Panama weaves with artisan-washed bands. Ornate scarves draped over open collars. The Artisan puts imperfection and intentional craft center stage.
Lead with Energy
Ties are back (+73% TikTok) in three directions: Modern, Ornate, and Prep. The flat-top cadet cap rises (+31% searches). Guardian-design bags with protective locks meet officewear resurgence (+146%). Black-and-gold hardware and RFID tech with air-tag integration define smart utility.
Build for Terrain
Plant-polymer bio-materials, Skinergy surfaces, and FSC-certified natural trims. Brimless UV-protective raffia hats and packable linen caps. Hyperfunctional belt bags and guardian crossbodies. Recovery slides and strappy sneakers. Earth & Tech bridges the trail to the lounge.
